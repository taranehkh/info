I am currently a data scientist at Microsoft Canada. Prior to Microsoft, I worked as a scientist at Zero Gravity Labs, the innovation and experimentation arm of LoyaltyOne. I hold a Ph.D. degree in Computer Science from the University of Western Ontario, where I focused on a variety of machine learning and statistical modelling methods in the context of natural language processing, social computing, and online privacy mining. Before joining Zero Gravity Labs, I worked at TD bank as a data scientist/engineer and at InfoTrellis as a machine learning researcher.
My primary areas of interests are in the fields of:

  • Machine Learning
  • Natural Language Processing
  • Social Computing
  • Information Visualization
  • Human Computer Interaction
